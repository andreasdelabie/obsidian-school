The Cornell method is a popular way of taking notes. In this Vault, I've created a base template that you can use.

- [ ] Create a note
- [ ] In the ribbon on the left, press `Insert template`
- [ ] Choose the `Cornell Note` option
- [ ] During class, take notes in the `# Notes` section
- [ ] When summarizing your notes, write down important terms in the `## Terms` section, and list potential exam questions in the `## Questions` section
- [ ] When you're finished summarizing, check the `completed` property at the top of the page
